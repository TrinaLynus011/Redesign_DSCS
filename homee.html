About Us

The Department of Data Science and Cyber Security at Karunya University is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in education and research in cutting-edge technologies. We offer state-of-the-art programs that combine theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for successful careers in the digital age.

Our department focuses on emerging areas like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, Cyber Security, and Blockchain Technology. With world-class faculty and industry collaborations, we provide students with the skills needed to excel in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in Data Science and Cyber Security education, fostering innovation and producing skilled professionals who can address the challenges of the digital age. We aim to create leaders who will shape the future of technology with integrity and vision.

Our Mission

To provide transformative education through innovative teaching methods, cutting-edge research, and strong industry collaborations. We are committed to developing professionals with strong ethical values who can contribute to technological advancements and societal well-being, while addressing the growing demands of the data-driven world.

Programs Offered

B.Tech Computer Engineering

A comprehensive program covering software development, algorithms, computer networks, and system design. Students gain hands-on experience with the latest technologies and programming paradigms through industry-aligned projects and internships.
